# Privacy Policy (pilot stub)

**OpsBoard AI** — landscaping operations software.

## Data we store

- Account email and name
- Organization and job data (customers, jobs, estimates, invoices)
- Activity and AI tool-call audit logs

## Hosting

Data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) in regions configured for your project.

## AI

Job context may be sent to Google Gemini to power the copilot. Do not include unnecessary sensitive data in prompts.

## Contact

Use in-app **Contact support** or your organization administrator.
