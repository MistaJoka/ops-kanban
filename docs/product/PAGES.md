# App Pages

## Public pages

### Landing
Explains the product and use case.

### Login
Email/password authentication.

### Signup
Creates user and organization workspace.

## App pages

### Dashboard
Executive control center.

Shows:

- active revenue
- unpaid invoices
- overdue cards
- today’s schedule
- bottlenecks
- risk alerts
- AI daily briefing

### Operations Board
Main workspace. This is the primary page.

Contains:

- universal Kanban pipeline
- cards
- column metrics
- AI command bar
- filters
- quick actions
- drag/drop movement

### Card Detail Modal
Deep operational record.

Contains:

- overview
- customer section
- job scope
- quote section
- scheduling section
- financial section
- files
- comments
- checklist
- timeline
- AI copilot panel

### Customers
CRM lens over card/customer data.

Contains:

- customer list
- customer profile
- linked cards
- past jobs
- invoices
- communication history

### Calendar
Scheduling lens over cards.

Contains:

- day view
- week view
- month view
- scheduled cards
- worker assignments
- conflicts

### Reports
Operational intelligence view.

Contains:

- pipeline velocity
- bottlenecks
- revenue forecast
- unpaid invoices
- conversion rate
- cycle time

### Notifications
Action center.

Contains:

- overdue alerts
- approvals
- AI action approvals
- payment reminders
- schedule conflicts

### Settings
Control center.

Contains:

- workspace settings
- users and roles
- pipeline columns
- custom fields
- automations
- integrations
- AI rules
- billing
